TapTrio

Tap, merge, and pop the numbers ✨

How to Play
  • Tap any tile to add +1 to its number.
  • Connect 3 or more matching numbers (up, down, left, right) and they merge into a higher tile.
  • Each tap spends energy; every merge gives some back. Run out of energy and the round ends.
  • Chain merges together for big combos and a higher score — then beat your personal best!
FAQ

Does TapTrio need an internet connection?

No. TapTrio is 100% offline. There is no account and no sign-in required.

Does it collect any of my data?

No. TapTrio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your high score is saved only on your device.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No ads and no in-app purchases.

My game isn't working / I found a bug.

Please email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll help as soon as we can.

Privacy

TapTrio is a fully offline, single-player game. It does not collect, use, or share any personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no network requests. Your highest score is stored locally on your device and is removed if you delete the app.
