TapTrio

Tap, merge, and pop the numbers ✨

How to Play
  • Tap any tile to add +1 to its number.
  • Connect 3 or more matching numbers (up, down, left, right) and they merge into a higher tile.
  • Each tap spends energy; every merge gives some back. When energy runs out, use your once-per-game revive to keep going.
  • Chain merges together for big combos and a higher score.
  • Sign in to Game Center to climb the global leaderboard and unlock achievements.
FAQ

Does TapTrio need an internet connection?

The game itself is fully playable offline. An internet connection is only used if you sign in to Apple's Game Center to sync the global leaderboard and achievements — and that is completely optional.

What is Game Center used for?

If you sign in to Game Center, TapTrio submits your best score to a global leaderboard and unlocks achievements. You can enjoy the full game without ever signing in.

Does it collect any of my data?

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data of its own. Your high score is saved on your device. Leaderboard and achievement data, if you use Game Center, are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No ads and no in-app purchases.

My game isn't working / I found a bug.

Please email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll help as soon as we can.

Privacy

TapTrio is a single-player game. The app itself does not collect, use, or share any personal information, and includes no third-party analytics, tracking, or ads. Your highest score is stored locally on your device. If you choose to sign in to Apple's Game Center, your scores and achievements are handled by Apple's Game Center service under Apple's Privacy Policy. TapTrio does not receive or store any personal data from Game Center.
